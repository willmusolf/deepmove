DeepMove

Chess improvement that starts with the games you actually played.

DeepMove is a chess review app built around a simple idea: the fastest way to improve is to study your own recurring mistakes, not just browse abstract advice. The product helps players import real games, inspect critical moments, compare alternatives, and turn a finished game into a practical study session.

What the product is for

Your games first

Import games from Chess.com, Lichess, or PGN and start with positions you actually reached.

Engine plus explanation

Use evaluation swings, best lines, and coaching context to understand why a move failed, not just that it failed.

Built for repetition

Review is meant to feed a repeatable study loop so the same tactical and strategic mistakes show up less often over time.

What a good review session looks like

  • Import a finished game from an external account or a PGN file.
  • Find the critical moments where the evaluation changed and the result truly turned.
  • Inspect best lines, move grades, and explanations to identify the underlying mistake pattern.
  • Take the lesson into the next game and repeat the process often enough that the habit changes.
